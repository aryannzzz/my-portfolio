Research Setting

StochLab at RBCCPS, IISc — led by Dr. Shishir N. Y. Kolathaya — focuses on stochastic and robust control for legged robots. The lab collaborates with Strider Robotics and Artpark on deploying quadruped platforms in unstructured outdoor environments. The internship runs 16 May – 15 July 2026 with a stipend of ₹10,000/month, formally offered by Dr. Kolathaya on 20 April 2026.

This internship sits at the intersection of RL policy training, physics simulation, and real-hardware deployment. The core challenge is the sim-to-real gap: policies that work perfectly in Isaac Sim often degrade on hardware due to unmodeled contact dynamics, actuator delays, and sensor noise.

Approach
Technical Work

Locomotion Policy Training Active

Training PPO-based locomotion controllers in NVIDIA Isaac Sim with domain randomization over terrain geometry, friction coefficients, body mass, and motor parameters. Curriculum starts from flat ground and progressively introduces stairs, slopes, and irregular surfaces.

Sim-to-Real Transfer

Applying actuator network modeling and latency injection to close the sim-to-real gap. Policy outputs are filtered through learned actuator models that account for motor response characteristics of the physical quadruped platform.

VLA Integration Active

Integrating vision-language action models for task-conditioned locomotion. The goal is to condition locomotion behaviour on natural language instructions — "navigate to the door", "avoid the obstacle" — bridging high-level planning and low-level motor control.

LeRobot Pipeline

Evaluating HuggingFace LeRobot for imitation learning baselines on manipulation-adjacent locomotion tasks. Comparing IL-trained policies against RL policies on generalization metrics across held-out terrain types.

Stack
Tools & Infrastructure

Isaac Sim for parallelized GPU simulation, ROS2 for hardware interface and sensor fusion, PyTorch for policy networks, and RSL-RL / custom PPO implementations for training. Domain randomization is implemented via Isaac's PhysX parameter randomization API.
